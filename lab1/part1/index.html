Attention!

This page will ask for your location data upon clicking the crosshair button in the top left corner of the map. This data is only used to display your approximate location on the map as well as the possible margin of error for your location. It also used to estimate the time of day at your location in order to automatically change the basemap style. As this is a school project, your location data is not stored, collected, or sent to third parties.
